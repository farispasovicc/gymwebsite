The fitness center "PROGYM" is a top-equipped, modernly designed fitness center. "PROGYM" offers the highest quality and most complete services in the field of fitness for both recreational and professional engagement in this type of sport.
You have access to friendly and professional staff who will help you exercise and give you advice during your training.
You also have the option to book private training sessions with one of the "PROGYM" instructors.
The spaces are well-ventilated with natural light, and additionally equipped with ventilation systems, air conditioning, and video surveillance. The fitness center also includes men's and women's locker rooms with showers and toilets.



The "PROGYM" fitness center is equipped with a line of machines “PRECOR”, which are among the best in the global fitness industry.
A high-quality cardio line, machines for all muscle groups, a large number of free weights, and other equipment allow you to achieve your desired results in the shortest possible time.
To see the quality of our gym and equipment, you can view pictures under "Photos".
